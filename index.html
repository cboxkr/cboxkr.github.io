JUNK?

You can download the subject of this research here.

We agree with the expert’s opinion given. We choose three points that we are impressed. First, we think that Brian Scudamore have virtues as entrepreneur. Those are pursuing his own faith and not being afraid of the risk. There are several kinds of investment. The most traditional thing is outsourcing. Outsourcing involves the contracting out of a business process to another party. It is used well because it minimizes the risk. But he didn’t take that way. He has his own faith which is to make the investment to people. We think highly of that point. The decision of him might give the risk to him but he believed himself and pursue his own faith. And that decision gave him success.

Second, we think company's image is clean and youth. Their job is taking garbage from private houses and other companies, so we can think their truck and clothes are could be dirty. But their truck is clean and they are uniformed to they make their image clean. And their mobile billboards, on-time service are have relation with youth and their culture mix in young, fun and focused on solid, healthy growth. We think clean image is similar with a Korea extermination of vermin company, SESCO. The business of these company is kill the bugs which in private houses and other companies. Their job is kill the buds but their equipment and staffs are clean to make image. These company’s image supported their success.

Third, to manage each franchise efficiently, this company operate the central call center. Comparing this point, there is a comparable venture company, CNT TECH in Korea. The founder of this company, Jeon Hwasung who was one of our team member’s individual business mentor, made a call center outsourcing company contacting with diverse food industry. With accumulated know-how, they produce differentiated high quality solution for operating and managing the call center efficiently. The objective data made by central call center such as sales report, time management, and customer information allows franchise partners to run their department efficiently. In addition, it increases the customer’s satisfaction and help the growth of market.
